Lasagna

One of the most loved foods in the world is finally here!

This is a beautiful Italian Lasagna with layers of slow cooked Ragù Bolognese

and Besciamella cheese sauce. Though patience is required, it is quite straight

forward to make as you will see in the recipe video!

Ragu Bolognese:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 medium carrot
  • 1 celery stick
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 kg / 2 lb beef mince
  • 800g / 28 oz canned crushed tomato
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste

Cheese Sauce (Besciamella):

  • 60g / 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 4 cups milk
  • 2 cups gruyere or Colby cheese

Lasagna:

  • 50g/ 12 oz fresh lasagna sheets
  • 1 1/2 cups (tightly packed) mozzarella cheese
  • Finely chopped basil or parsley
